About Me

My greatest passion in life is learning new things and learning them well. For the past 10 years I have self-taught myself how to build websites, HTML basics, SEO best practices, content writing and design, and I love creating things with Adobe products and Figma. I realized I could bring my life experience into something tangible for others by creating beautiful web and content design that gives a voice to what people want to express, create products that work as an extension of human interaction, and that bring ease to the user experience by listening closely to what humans need to help make their life graceful.
